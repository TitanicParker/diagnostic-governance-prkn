Ethical note

The purpose is analysis, not reputational attack.

The patient is the author and subject of this archive. Redaction is used to protect dignity, privacy, third parties, and non-essential identifying information. The site distinguishes documentary fact, patient interpretation, ethical analysis, and unresolved questions.
