This is a theory that probably applies to most people who actually develop Parkinson's disease “sporadically” or “idiopathically” who may have susceptibility genes that we do not understand the role of as yet or known genes with variations (e.g. In the LRRK2/ SNCA / GBA genes), as well as a range of possible environmental factors. #3---what I term for [Patient]’s understandinf as "weak genetics" ---by this we mean the classical autosomal recessively inherited disorders that can occur within a family but which would be very unlikely to affect other family members.
